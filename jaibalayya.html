K L University



KL University is a private university located in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was founded in 1980 by Koneru Lakshmaiah Charity Foundation. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, science, and humanities.


KL University is one of the leading universities in India. It has been ranked among the top 100 universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in recent years. The university is also accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an A+ grade.


KL University has a strong focus on research and innovation. It has over 100 research centers and laboratories. The university has also filed over 1000 patents. KL University has produced many successful alumni, including entrepreneurs, CEOs, and government officials.
