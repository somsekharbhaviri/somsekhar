About SAMYAK

SAMYAK is a prestigious national-level techno-management fest held annually at K L University. It brings together a diverse and talented group of individuals, including budding engineers, aspiring managers, and entrepreneurs.

The event serves as a platform where dreams and skills intertwine to create a tapestry of innovation. SAMYAK is adorned with an exciting array of technical and literary competitions, trend-setting campaigns, skill-enhancing workshops, and enthralling cultural shows. It has earned its reputation as the crown jewel of college festivals in Andhra Pradesh.

In its sixth edition, SAMYAK embraces the theme of "MAKE IN INDIA," reflecting the call to unleash audacious ideas and nurture the nation's talent. It's a time to contribute to the exponential growth of India's economy, education, research, and infrastructure.
